Category I Magic-User Spells Levels 1–6 · 72 Spells
How MU spells work: A Magic-User begins with a spellbook containing 2 starting spells (plus Read Magic). New spells must be found as loot or copied from other books — there is no automatic list. The MU memorises any combination of known spells up to their daily slots, then expends them on casting. At Basic level (1–3), MUs cast Level 1–2 spells. Expert (4–14) opens Levels 3–6. The scarcity of spells is a core design feature, not a flaw.
Level 1 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 1 (1 slot). Most reliable spells in the MU's arsenal — utility and encounter-control. Sleep alone can define low-level play.
Level 2 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 3 (1 slot). Utility expands dramatically. Invisibility and Web redefine tactical options.
Level 3 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 5. Fireball and Lightning Bolt arrive. Fly opens vertical play entirely.
Level 4 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 7. Permanent transformation, large-scale terrain effects, and battlefield mastery.
Level 5 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 9. High-risk, high-reward spells. Teleport and Conjure Elemental define high-level MU play.
Level 6 — 12 Spells
Available from Level 11. Campaign-scale powers. Disintegrate, Death Spell, and Geas operate beyond combat.
Category II Cleric Spells Levels 1–5 · 38 Spells
How Cleric spells work: A Cleric prays for any spells from the full available list each morning — they are not limited to known spells. A 1st-level Cleric has no spell slots (spells begin at 2nd level). Reversed spells (italicised in the effect) require a Chaotic alignment or referee permission for Lawful Clerics. The Cleric's breadth of utility — healing, protection, information, and reversal options — makes them the most flexible low-level caster.
Level 1 — 8 Spells
Available from Level 2 (1 slot). Healing, protection, and elemental resistance form the foundation.
Level 2 — 8 Spells
Available from Level 3. Bless is a mass combat buff. Silence and Hold Person give the Cleric offensive reach.
Level 3 — 7 Spells
Available from Level 5. Cure Disease is the Mummy Rot lifeline. Speak with Dead opens investigation. Remove Curse arrives one level earlier than the MU version.
Level 4 — 7 Spells
Available from Level 7. Neutralize Poison is a time-critical death-reversal. Create Water solves wilderness supply.
Level 5 — 8 Spells
Available from Level 9. Raise Dead and Word of Recall define the high-level Cleric. Commune and Dispel Evil are campaign-scale tools.
